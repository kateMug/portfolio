Psychotherapy In Auckland

This was a re-design for a Psychotherapy business in Ponsonby. Key requirements for this website were that it was easy to navigate around, that is was mobile first, that there was a way to find questions and answers around the process of Psychotherapy. The logo and colour scheme was also re-designed for this site.

The website was built with Sass on a Bootstrap framework. Added features included a subject search, hidden information pages, image carousel, question and answers page in an accordian and google map. SEO and directory listings, google adwords and a facebook campaign that increased traffic to the site.

cv

I have worked in art galleries and Museums for over ten years. During this time I have helped to maintain and develop websites to varying degrees. I found I enjoyed this aspect of my job so much I decided to make it my full time career.

I have also made a number of websites for various Art Events and for small to medium sized businesses.

I enjoy the visual aspect of web design, having good visual appeal is crucial to any website. I have studied back-end web development for 2 years and recently graduated with a Graduate Diploma in Creative Technologies at Media Design School.

It is the code behind what you see that makes a website work really smoothly. Having an enjoyable user experience is what keeps people coming back to your site and my goal for every website I do is to make it engaging.

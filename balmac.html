The client requested a warm, friendly and down to earth feel. No7Balmac is a local favourite with a loyal client base. This site was designed to appeal to existing customers, tourists and out of towners. Events and socal media links were important elements, as was a clear map and an easy to use and inviting booking form.

This site is currently in construction for a client in Dunedin wanting to update their existing website. The site was built with Sass and will be pulled into a wordpress framework with php functionality. An Eveve booking form will be incorporated for live bookings. Some elements were animated to give a fun and friendly vibe, fitting with the clients brief

The client requested a hamburger menu for the mobile site while a slider menu was incorporated for the tablet and desktop versions.

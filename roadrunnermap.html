Roadrunner is a concept site which examines New Zealand's road fatalities and injuries. The site aims to highlight road statistics through interactive graphs and maps to engage and inform users and encourange good driver behaviour.

This concept site is currently in construction. Key features include a live 'crash map' that records driver crashes throughout New Zealand and warns drivers of dangerous areas. Information is based on statistics from the NZTA CASS database.

Interactive maps like this one uses road stats in a more illustrative way. This was taken from a spreadsheet from the NZTA website.
